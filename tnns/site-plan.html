Content

Home page

Welcome to Tennis Connect! Whether you're a seasoned pro or just picking up a racket, we’re here to bring tennis lovers together. Our goal is to create a community where players of all levels can connect, learn, and grow the game. Looking to practice with a group, find a local tournament, or get started on your tennis journey? Tennis Connect has everything you need. Let’s hit the courts, share the passion, and enjoy the game together! Tennis is a dynamic sport that combines physical fitness, mental strategy, and social interaction—all in one game. Whether you're looking for a great cardiovascular workout, mental stimulation, or an opportunity to make new friends, tennis offers it all. It’s a lifelong sport that can be enjoyed at any age, with a low risk of injury and plenty of opportunities for both casual play and competitive matches. At Tennis Connect, we believe in the power of tennis to enrich lives and bring people together.

Event Calendar

Upcoming Events Stay up to date with all our upcoming events! Don’t miss out on the next local tournament or special group practice session. Check out the calendar below for details on times, dates, and how to sign up: November 2024 - Group Practice Sessions: Monday–Friday, 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM SIGN UP HERE - Monthly Singles Tournament: November 20th, 9:00 AM SIGN UP HERE - Monthly Mixed Doubles Tournament: November 25th, 9:00 AM SIGN UP HERE Group Practice Sessions Join us for group practice Monday through Friday, where players of all levels can improve their skills in a structured, supportive environment. Each session includes focused drills for forehand and backhand techniques, serving practice, and fast-paced, competitive short games. Participants are encouraged to bring their own racket, while tennis balls will be provided. Whether you're looking to refine your strokes or build match fitness, our group practices are the perfect way to take your game to the next level. Local Tournaments Ready to relive your glory days on the court? We host organized tennis tournaments every month, offering competitive play for all skill levels. Our tournaments feature the following categories: Boys' Singles, Girls' Singles, Boys' Doubles, Girls' Doubles, and Mixed Doubles. All tournaments are structured as single-elimination events, ensuring exciting, high-stakes matches from start to finish. Join us for your chance to compete, improve, and showcase your skills!

Private Lessons

Tennis Lessons Our tennis lessons are designed for beginners, offering a solid foundation in the fundamentals of the game. If you’ve played before and are looking to refine your skills, we recommend joining our group practice sessions. Lessons focus on key areas such as forehand and backhand drills, serving technique, and understanding the rules of tennis. Lessons are available by appointment and can be scheduled by contacting our instructor via email. The cost is $15 per hour for a private lesson, or you can bring a friend—each additional participant is just $2 extra. Whether you prefer one-on-one coaching or a shared experience, our lessons provide personalized attention to help you improve your game.
